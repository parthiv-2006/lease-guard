*AdjournmentRequest*
**
**

**
2. There was no basis for
M.S. to assume that no other matters would be scheduled for thesame hearing block as the present
application.As well, M.S. had
indicated to theLandlord and Tenant
Board (the ‘Board’) that he was available to participate in thehearing on May 5, 2021 and did not mention

2021.Instead, he simply presumed
that the Boardwould accommodate his
schedule on the date of the hearing and gambled that no othermatters would proceed during the hearing
block.As a result, the adjournment
requestwas denied.
